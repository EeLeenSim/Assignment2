Does Quality of Life comes with a Price?

Due to the Lebanese liquidity crisis, an ongoing financial crisis affecting Lebanon, the country has high living index as the country mainly depends on import goods. The crisis makes the people want to emigrate and among those leaving are doctors. The WHO has said most hospitals are operating at 50% capacity, around 40% of doctors and 30% of nurses have permanently emigrated and causing the factor health to decrease drastically.
