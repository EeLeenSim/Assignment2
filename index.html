How is Quality of Life Measured?

A total of 36 factors were included in the calculation of the overall index, which were divided into 7 subject areas here. The best achievable value in each division is 100. Stability considered the sector of economic and politics of a country. Political stability does not necessary contribute to civil rights if it comes from dictatorial principles. Besides, health services is also an essential element that contribute to quality of life. As for safety, it is also crucial as even the most beautiful country cannot satisfy anyone if their life is in danger. A pleasant climate takes in account of number of hours of sunshine and the humidity. Cost of living is the most important factor as ones must be able to afford for having quality to experience best conditions. This factor considered the average annual income and income tax rates. A country's popularity is evaluated by migration rate and number of tourists.

Does Quality of Life comes with a Price?

Due to the Lebanese liquidity crisis, an ongoing financial crisis affecting Lebanon, the country has high living index as the country mainly depends on import goods. The crisis makes the people want to emigrate and among those leaving are doctors. The WHO has said most hospitals are operating at 50% capacity, around 40% of doctors and 30% of nurses have permanently emigrated and causing the factor health to decrease drastically.
